How It Works
1
Sign in with Google — authorise this app to send emails via your Gmail account using OAuth 2.0.
2
Select recipients — all 60+ prosthodontists are pre-loaded. Deselect any you don't want to include.
3
Attach the PDF — upload the APSA Day 2026 programme PDF to include as an attachment.
4
Send — each email is personalised with the recipient's full name and sent one at a time with a small delay to avoid spam filters.
